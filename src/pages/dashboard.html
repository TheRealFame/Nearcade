Community STUN Servers

Browse and select public STUN servers hosted by the community.

What is a STUN server?
A STUN server is a tiny relay used by WebRTC strictly to discover your public IP address so you can establish a direct Peer-to-Peer connection with other players. It does not route your video, audio, or game inputs.

Privacy Warning:
If you select a Community STUN Server, the person hosting that server will be able to see your public IP address. They cannot see or intercept any game data, but they will know your IP address connected to them.

Community TURN Servers

Browse and select public TURN servers hosted by the community.

What is a TURN server?
A TURN server is a high-bandwidth relay used by WebRTC when a direct Peer-to-Peer connection fails. It routes ALL of your video, audio, and game input traffic.

CRITICAL PRIVACY WARNING:
If you select a Community TURN Server, the person hosting that server will be routing your entire game stream. While WebRTC is end-to-end encrypted (meaning they cannot decrypt your video/audio or intercept passwords), they can see your public IP address and measure your exact bandwidth usage. Only select TURN servers from hosts you trust.
